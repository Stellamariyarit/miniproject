Why Organ Donation Matters

Organ donation is a life-saving gift. Thousands of people die every year waiting for an organ transplant. By registering as an organ donor, you can help save up to eight lives. Donated organs can include the heart, lungs, liver, kidneys, and pancreas.

Did You Know?

  • One organ donor can save up to 8 lives.
  • Millions of people are currently on organ transplant waiting lists.
  • Most people can become organ donors, regardless of age or health history.
  • Registering to be an organ donor takes only a few minutes.
